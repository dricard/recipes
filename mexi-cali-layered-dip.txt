Mexi-Cali Layered Dip

This party-perfect appetizer teams the flavors of Mexican and California cuisine in a lively dip with a bright PACE picante sauce topping.

1 ripe avocado, peeled, seeded and mashed
1 cup PACE picante sauce
1 teaspoon lemon juice
¼ teaspoon salt
½ medium tomato, chopped
½ cup thinly sliced ripe olives
¾ cup dairy sour cream
½ cup (2 ounces) shredded cheddar or monterey jack cheese

Combine avocado, 2 tablespoons of picante sauce, lemon juice and salt; mix well.

Stir in tomato.

Spoon evenly onto bottom of shallow 1-quart clear glass bowl (straight sides preferred) or 8-inch pie plate.

Top with olives; cover and chill.

To serve, spread sour cream; sprinkle with cheese.

Serve with tortilla chips or corn chips.

Makes about 3½ cups.
